What it is

A crunchy oat crust “cup” that you fill with yogurt (or a thick topping), then finish with fruit or date pieces.

Ingredients

Rolled oats • Peanut butter • Coconut oil • Maple syrup • Chia • Flax • Hemp • Cinnamon

Tip: keep them airtight to protect the crunch.

Best for

  • Busy mornings
  • Pre / post-workout snack
  • Light “dessert” that feels clean

How to enjoy

  • Classic: Greek yogurt + chopped date pieces
  • Fresh: Yogurt + berries (or dried fruit)
  • Energy: Nut butter + date pieces
  • Dessert vibe: Yogurt + cocoa dust + dates
