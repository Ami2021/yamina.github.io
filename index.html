What it is

A crisp oat “crust” you can fill like a mini bowl. It’s meant to be crunchy—not soft—so it holds up with yogurt.

Ingredients: Rolled oats, peanut butter, coconut oil, maple syrup, chia seeds, flax seeds, hemp seeds.
Allergens: Contains peanuts.
Made locally. No additives.
  • Best for: busy mornings, snack breaks, pre/post-workout, light dessert
  • Texture: crunchy & sturdy (designed to hold yogurt)
  • Serving: fill + top + eat — no prep needed

How to enjoy

Simple combos that make it feel like a treat (without being heavy).

  • Fill with yogurt + top with fruit (or date pieces)
  • Add nut butter or a spoon of jam
  • Sprinkle cinnamon (optional)
